CSE Inno'Bits

AVS Engineering College's Computer Science and Engineering department is excited to host a National-level Technical symposium. The event features compelling technical competitions like Paper Presentation, focusing on emerging technologies such as AI/ML, Big Data, Blockchain, Quantum Computing, and IoT. Join and explore the potential impact of these transformative technologies through Project Expo.

In addition to the technical competitions, the symposium offers an array of non-technical events. Mark your calendar for this exciting event that ensures to blend innovation, knowledge, and fun.

Paper Presentation

Our Paper Presentation event focuses on emerging technologies that are shaping the future. Participants will delve into topics such as Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning, Big Data, Blockchain, Quantum Computing, and the Internet of Things (IoT) Which discusses the potential impact of these transformative technologies.

Project Expo

Unveil your innovative and unique projects at our Expo. Share your inventive Projects with fellow enthusiasts. Get a chance to win exciting prizes for the most outstanding and unique expo entries. Make sure not to miss this opportunity to shine and be recognized for your creativity and ingenuity!

  • Venue: COE Lab, CSE Main Block
